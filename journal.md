<h2>1.5h - Made Circut design in falstad</h2>
I got the idea of making 4 consecutive blinking LEDs after seeing the astable multivibrator in the tutorial. I started by putting the circut into falstad, and duplicating it, tweaking some bits so that there was only one power source and each LED was connected to an adjacent one. A challenge I faced was dificulty with connections between the 4 LED subcircits, and how to connect then together to all take power from one source, and blink one after another, the timing.
